! Speed up your webbrowsing or save data on your data plan with a few rules.

! Blocks beacons
||*$other,third-party

! Will break some sites
||*$font,third-party

! Let's stop websocket requests from 3rd parties
||*$websocket,third-party

! Block document request's from 3rd parties
||*$document,third-party

! Blocks minor advertisers
/js/ads.

! I don't know why. But I rarely see (static) domains do XHR requests, so better add it here.
||*static.*$xmlhttprequest,third-party

! Can block shell's that has managed to escape into unusual parts of a site
/css/images/*$script
/uploads/images/*$script
